YOGA PASSION

Feel free and explore my portfolio to get a glimpse of my style and artistry. Is has been two years of practising and strenghten my passion for Yoga, connecting my fascination for the human body with it’s physical nature and sheer endless capabilities. Yoga not only keeps my body fit but enrichens my mind and resistance day by day. Soon I decided to share my experiences with others to spread my love for Yoga and people, that began with my first Yoga teacher training in India in August 2017. Currently I’m preparing workshops and classes to cocreate and express our selves together. Feel free, stay tuned and reach out!
